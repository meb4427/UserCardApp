Search for the Page you'd like to post on, then select it from the dropdown menu. Click on 'Start', then 'Edit' at the bottom. You can then enter your keywords and other related information. Then, click 'Next', and all the rest of the typing should be done for you. Click on 'Finish' to post the content. Your profile also contains information about your interests. If you can find a link that relates to a particular interest of yours, link to it in your profile.
Log into Facebook on a computer. From your News Feed, click Friends in the left menu. Then click on a person to see posts, photos and stories that person is tagged in and have previously shown you. Click on a person’s name and search for another person.
Tap in the top right of Facebook, then tap your name. Tap your profile picture then tap Change Profile Picture. Your profile picture will now show with a gradient background. Change your profile picture as many times as you like for free. To make your profile picture into a smiley face, you will need to buy a new profile picture from Facebook. There is also an option to remove the profile picture you've already set on Facebook.
Where can I find my Facebook settings? Go to Facebook settings. Click on “Apps” on the left-hand side. Then scroll down and click on "Manage apps." Click on the app you want to access.
